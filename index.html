Learn More Vocabulary With Alexa

Voice Lexicon enables you to play your vocabulary on Alexa and helps enforce your learning

About

Why

Learning new vocabulary is a demanding process and it is even more daunting for those who suffer from learning disability. One of the ways to increase the efficiency of learning is the multi-sensory approach. That is why we want to improve the accessibility of vocabulary in audio format and make it available on Alexa.

How

We used Amazon Polly text-to-speech technology to generate a multi-language audio file from your vocabulary and make it available through Voice Lexicon skill with provided phrase on any of the 50 million available Alexa devices.
