Learn Vocabularies With Alexa

Give us your vocabularies and translation, and we will make your Alexa play it for you to enforce your learning

About

Why

Learning new vocabularies is a demanding process and even more for people with dyslexia. The multisensory approach helps to increase the effectivity of learning. That is why we want to improve the accessibility of vocabularies in audio format significantly.

How

We use Amazon Polly text-to-speech technology to generate an audio file from your vocabularies and make it available on 50 million Alexa devices with the phrases we provide you with.
